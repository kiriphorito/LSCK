Progress


In this page we will talk what progress and issues that we have had over the first and last terms with COMP204P and COMP205P along with bi-weekly reports and other documents.

Overall

In the first term of the project past term, the aim of the team for this project was to:

  • Meet up with client
  • Establish the requirements with the client
  • Have done some initial research
  • With the research, experiment developing some of the features and requirements
  • Come up with a design and prototype for the extension and the website
  • Create a website to promote the project and document it

In the second term, the aim was to:

  • Start on the implementation by using the experiments and joining them into one app
  • Start manual testing for integrated testing
  • Have a final proof of concept
  • Finsh the project website will all content

Imagine Cup 2017

Since our client is Microsoft and our project was designed with Microsoft Visual Studio in mind. We decided that it would be good to submit our project is Microsoft's Imagine Cup 2017. Our team name is Javisam and our project title is Lightweight Start-up Company Kit (LSCK).

Set-Backs and Issues

As a team, we have had a number of set-backs which has led us to feel that we are behind the schedule. Firstly, we only have two members in this project rather than three and also we didn’t manage to meet our client until the 8th week of the project. It was only then we found that our initial assumptions from the brief given were wrong making our experiments, research and prototypes where completely incorrect.

Given these setbacks, we as a team are still moving ahead to get this project completed.
